About WeCare

WeCare is a non-profit Organization founded by four budding Zimbabwean technocrats, who are passionate about making a positive impact in all communities by striving to achieve or attain lasting solutions to an already dire situation within the communities in the face of difficulty and resistance.Our mission is to be the helping-hand for those who find themselves faced with dilemma's that could ultimately take a toll on them if assistance is not readily accessible.

Are you a single mother?

Being a single mother can be a very daunting task. The first step is to acknowledge that having to raise kids as a single parent is not ideal. However there are a few things that you can do to get started on the right path. One of those things is, "TO GET INFORMATION"!

Whatever you do just know that you are not alone and we are here to help in any way possible!

having trouble dealing with drugs and alcohol?

It is not practically possible to prevent people from taking drugs, but we can all take practial steps to help others, whether they are friends, family members, workmates or neighbours to stop substance abuse. By sharing this piece of information with others, we can all help somebody to avoid drug abuse or help somebody to stop indulging in drugs altogether. Substance abuse is partcularly rife in teenage youth and can negatively affect their education, careers and even relationships if left unaddressed. substance abuse comes in many forms: alcohol abuse; marijuana; sniffing glue; illicit drugs like broncleer etc.
